Creating xBD: A dataset for assessing building damage from satellite imagery

Image | General | Response

xBD is a general-disaster dataset for change detection and damage assessment of buildings. It contains 700,000 labeled building instances in over 5,000 km^2 of imagery. It is introduced for multiclass (ordinal) classification.

  • ML task type: Multiclass (ordinal) classification
  • Data Source: Earth Observation Data and GeoSpatial Imagery (Satellite)
  • Size: 700,000 labeled building instances in over 5,000 km^2 of imagery
  • Timespan: 2010 - 2019
  • Geographical Coverage: Global
  • Published: 2019
