This site presents a collection of existing benchmark datasets for machine learning models for natural disasters. The supported features and individual dataset information are specifically included as per feedback collected through user interviews. Our goal is to facilitate the proccess of searchig for natural disaster datasets for ML engineers.

NaD Benchmarks 2 is an extention of Benchmark datasets for Machine Learning for Natural Disasters as introduced by Proma et al.
